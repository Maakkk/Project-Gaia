How to Help

We are selling merchandise to spread awareness of the negative impact we have on the Earth. All of our funds will be given to the Environmental Defense Fund in order to help them, help us. The Environmental Defense Fund takes on climate change and other grave threats to energy, ecosystems, oceans, and health by using evidence and partnerships to take legal action. Donate to the EDF to support the rehabilitation of a better Earth.
